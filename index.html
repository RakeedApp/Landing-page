Automated Price Dropping

Prices automatically decrease over time until the product is sold. Sellers set a starting price and minimum, then watch inventory move as prices drop. Buyers get better deals the longer they wait.

نزول السعر الآلي

الأسعار تنزل تلقائياً مع الوقت لين ينباع المنتج. البياعة يحددون سعر البداية والحد الأدنى، وبعدين يشوفون المخزون يتحرك مع نزول الأسعار

Live Auctions

Participate in real-time auctions with competitive bidding. Watch prices rise as buyers compete. Get instant notifications and never miss your chance to bid on items you want.
